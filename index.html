Short Bio

I received Ph.D. degree of Computer Science at Texas A&M University, advised by Dr. Xia Ben Hu. My research interests are Retrieval Augmentated Generation, Recommendation Systems, Defect Detection, Hyperparameter Optimization, Neural Architecture Search, Distribution System, and Label Noise.

  • I currently work in Microsoft as a machine learning engineer since Jan. 2023, focusing on recommendation systems and retrieval augmentated generation (RAG).
  • I was an Applied Scientist Intern at Amazon in Fall 2021, working on automated video generation for video ads with Dr. Chu Wang.
  • I was a Research Intern at Microsoft in Summer 2021, working on constrained hyperparameter optimization with Dr. Chi Wang.

Before that, I obtained M.S. degree in Computer Science and Information Engineering at National Taiwan University (NTU) in 2015. My advisor was Dr. Shih-Hao Hung.
