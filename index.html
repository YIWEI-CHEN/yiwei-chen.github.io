Short Bio

I received Ph.D. degree of Computer Science at Texas A&M University, advised by Dr. Xia Ben Hu. I currently work in Microsoft as a machine learning engineer, focusing on recommendation systems and retrieval augmentated generation (RAG). I was an Applied Scientist Intern at Amazon in Fall 2021, working on automated video generation for video ads with Dr. Chu Wang. Moreover, I was a Research Intern at Microsoft in Summer 2021, working on constrained hyperparameter optimization with Dr. Chi Wang. My current research interests are Defect Detection, Hyperparameter Optimization, Neural Architecture Search, Distribution System, and Label Noise.

Before that, I obtained M.S. degree in Computer Science and Information Engineering at National Taiwan University (NTU) in 2015. My advisor was Dr. Shih-Hao Hung.
